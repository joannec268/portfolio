A Little About Me...

Hey! I’m Joanne. I'm a student majoring in Design at the University of California, Davis with a focus on UI/UX design.

I am fascinated by the intersection of design and human behavior. I believe that understanding how people feel and interact with technology is essential in creating meaningful user-centered designs that not only solve problems but also bring efficiency and engagement to my users.

When I am not designing, I enjoy crocheting and spending time with my Corgi, who is the inspiration for my logo, which is a blend of her little face with my initials!
